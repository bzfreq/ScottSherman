About

Scotty Sherman blends stand-up, performance art, and emotional misfires into a set that feels half-confessional, half-hostage situation. The comedy lives in discomfort — and in committing fully to it.

Style

Expect long silences, sudden sincerity, and moments that feel like they might collapse — until they don’t. Scotty doesn’t chase punchlines; he lets the audience sit in the tension.
